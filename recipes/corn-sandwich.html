Corn Sandwich

A corn sandwich is a toasted sandwich filled with sweet corn, cheese, and bell peppers, grilled in buttered bread until crisp and golden.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Corn
  • Cheese
  • Butter
  • Bell pepper

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the sweet corn, cheese and bell peppers.
  2. Spread softened butter on the outside of each slice of bread.
  3. Spread the corn mixture on one slice of bread, then cover with the other slice.
  4. Cook on a pan over medium heat, pressing lightly, until both sides are golden brown and crispy.
