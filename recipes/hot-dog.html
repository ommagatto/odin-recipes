Hot dog

A Hot dog is a grilled sausage in a soft bun topped with ketchup, mustard, and whatever else you like. Dogs not included.

Ingredients

  • Hot dog bun
  • Sausage
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Cook the sausage until browned and heated through.
  2. Warm or lightly toast the hot dog bun.
  3. Place the cooked sausage inside the bun.
  4. Spread mayonnaise over the sausage.
  5. Add ketchup and mustard to taste.
